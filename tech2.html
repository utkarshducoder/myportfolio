Is Vivo Y15 worth the money or is it just a hype?

Vivo Y15 is a recent variant of the Vivo Y-series. Vivo unveiled on 28 May 2019. Since then, there is much cry on the internet, if this is a better choice to buy or not. Are you are looking forward to investing in a smartphone which provides the best user experience with classy look?Then this is a promising one. Its prime focus is on photography and long-hours of usability. These features are the deal breakers for most of the customers. If you are on a budget, Vivo Y15 offers In-budget pricing of Rs. 12,990. It is available on Flipkart, Amazon, Paytm and Snapdeal and the offline market. This smartphone is one of the best among the phones under 15,000. Are you having second thoughts on buying Vivo Y15? Here are the features that the smartphone offers.


Vivo Y15 runs on Android 9.0. It provides you with a Ram of 4 GB and 64 GB of Rom so, there will be no sign of lag even if you use a memory consuming application. You get two different color options to choose Burgundy Red and Aqua Blue. The smartphone has a plastic building material on its back. Connectivity options it provides are 3G, 4G, GPS, Wi-Fi of 2.5GHz, Bluetooth 5.0, FM radio, headphone jack of 3.5 mm and a UBS port. For storage, the smartphone has 64 GB of internal storage and can expand to 256 GB using a micro-SD card. Vivo Y15 functions on an octa-core MediaTek MT6762 Helio P22 (12nm) processor and makes it Super-fast and fluent. It has a powerful 5000 mAh battery that comes with intelligent battery saving technology. It comes with a Full view display with a 19.3:9 aspect ratio and a touchscreen size of 6.35 inches (16.15 cm) with an IPS Display. It has a resolution of 720 1544 at 268 PPI. This technology checks the speedy performance that Vivo Y15 offers. Super Nano bezel stretches the screen-to-ratio to 89% on all sides with a waterproof notch.


Face detection and the fingerprint sensor are supported in this device. It supports other sensors like Ambient Light Sensor, Rear fingerprint sensor, Proximity sensor, Accelerometer, Compass, Gyroscope. Besides, it has a dual sim slot (GSM+GSM), OTG support. Hence, Vivo Y15 is a perfect fit for the upgraded devices in Vivo Y-series. A phone with a smart look, seamless performance, best-in-class, an excellent battery, and also within the range of under 15,000. It has a massive battery capacity and a great camera. We hope this would have helped you to reach your favourable conclusion. So, Vivo Y15 is absolutely money worthy.
